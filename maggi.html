Maggi Recipe

Description

It originated in Switzerland in the late 19th century. The brand was founded by Swiss entrepreneur Julius Maggi. While Maggi is widely popular and a household name in India, it is a brand of the Swiss multinational company Nestlé. Nestlé introduced Maggi noodles to the Indian market in 1982.

Ingredients:

  • Maggi Noodles
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Oil

Steps to follow:

  • In a saucepan, pour 1½ cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  • If you're using vegetables, add them first and let them cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Break the Maggi noodle cake into 2-3 pieces and add it to the boiling water
  • Stir well and let it cook for 2-3 minutes on medium heat, until the water is mostly absorbed and the noodles are soft.
