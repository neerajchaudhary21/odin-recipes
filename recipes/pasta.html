Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • salt
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Steps to follow:

  • Mix the ingredients
  • Form a Dough ball
  • Knead the dough
  • form into balls
  • Roll the pasta
  • Roll the dough again
  • Cut the dough into pasta
  • Dry the pasta
  • Use a rolling pin
